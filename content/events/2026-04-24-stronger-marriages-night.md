Your marriage matters.  It matters to you, to others, even to your community. We believe God wants your marriage to be strong; to be full of joy and vitality, to be healthy and rich, to be a blessing to you and those in your life.  We believe that your marriage is important, and that it is worth investing in.  And as everyone who’s married can attest, marriage is challenging and we all need encouragement and help from time to time. 

  
Please join us for a night devoted to empowering strong marriages. You’ll hear briefly from our staff before Ken Howard, an expert counselor and speaker in the field leads our evening. We are confident you’ll leave encouraged and energized for the year ahead.
